About Attendance !

  • This is a WebApp specifically designed for a final year Skripsie at the University of Stellenbosch in October 2020.

  • Registering

    Make sure your user name is your UT Number that appears on your university staff-access card (US-card).

    This number should appear on both the scanner and on this website. Only you will have access to the attendance data you have uploaded.

  • How to use the attendance scanner

    1. First switch on the Scanner device and log in by simply scanning your US-card.

    2. Navigate to the 'Scan Cards' window to start a Session and let students scan their cards as they enter the classroom. (As soon as the 'Scan Cards' window opens, you have started a new session. When you press 'BACK' the session ends. Keep this in mind to help manage your sessions.)

    3. When you want to retreive the data, navigate to the 'Export' window on the Scanner device and plug in a USB flash drive into the Scanner's USB port. Click on 'You can then take the USB memory device to transfer the data to a computer with access to the SUCS website.

  • How to use the attendance scanner

    1. Log in with your US-number (username) and password.

    2. Navigate to the Home page and select 'Upload Attendance'. Click on 'Choose File', then select the file exported by the Scanner device and click 'Upload'.

    You will then be able to see the logs on the Home page.
